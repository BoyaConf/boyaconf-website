< BoyaConf >

November 9th, 2019

Duitama, Boyacá, Colombia
BoyaConf is an initiative from a group of students from Boyacá, Colombia 🇨🇴, who - inspired and motivated by the multiple software development communities in Colombia - want to organize the very first conference of its kind in this Colombian state.

We want to allow sharing knowledge and deepen in IT industry related topics in order to create a recognized local community.
Our mission is to increase knowledge level of the Boyacá community. We plan to do this by being a programming language agnostic conference, which allows us to cover a wide range of topics such as:
Functional Programming, Machine Learning, DevOps, Microservices, Multiplatform Development, Internet of Things, Cloud Computing, Information Security, Continuous Deployment and Integration, Tips for Juniors, Soft Skills, and more.
